Baratha Samskara Pracharana Sabha

Spiritual Mental and Physical well being of a Person

  • Spiritual Health Awareness
  • Mental Health Awareness
  • Physical Health Awareness

Your children are not your children. They are sons and daughters of the Almighty. They come through you but not from you, and though they are with you yet they do not belong to you.

You may give them your love but not your thoughts and ideas, for they have their own thoughts and ideas. You may house their bodies but not their spirit and souls. Their souls dwell in the plan and purpose of the Almighty, which you cannot even visualize in your dreams. You may strive to be like them but seek not to make them like you.

We help them to understand the plan and purpose of their life that has been designed by the Almighty, and train them to attain the same. We help them to nurture their thoughts to develop good deeds and thereby develop their character to have good habits. we help them to develop a healthy body to have optimal strength and fitness.

As our own WHO (World Health Organization) defines; Optimal Health is a state of complete physical, mental and social well-being and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity.

From the age of three a child is given literacy education. Children who enter the books open the world of letters and the world of knowledge. But there are many things that children need to know, observe and practice in addition to the world of letters and knowledge they acquire from within books at each stage of their development. Here are the few necessary training programs and routines for their full physical, mental and spiritual growth

Some of the training topics to the kids

  • Hygiene and Body Caring
  • Daily Life Habits
  • Behaviour Development Training
  • Ability to Eligibility
  • Self Esteem and Body Imagee
  • Mental & Physical Health
  • Addiction Free Mind Training
Physical

Food is not just some filling substances to subside hunger, and exercise is not the artificial process of giving strain to body parts.

Mental

Feeding a lot of knowledge about various and numerous topics is not sufficient for the well-being of good mental health.

Spiritual

Traditional religious belief systems and ritual activities are for mental satisfaction not for spiritual satisfaction or well being.
