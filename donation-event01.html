Love your neighbor as yourself!

19th March 2023 - 31st March 2023 Poovar, Thiruvananthapuram

Dears,

Being children of one God all of us are siblings. Considering this truthful fact I would like to introduce one of our helpless sisters lives in Pune, who really deserves our concern & co-operation. Husband diowned her:-left alone, ran to faraway place somewhere in an intention for enjoying self-centered life in his own likes & pleasures,- while their coupled life had to face severe tragic condition of domestic situations. This lady's old & sick parents are also staying with her. They are fully dependants as they are now in a stage to be cared & looked after by somebody else. She has 2 children in studentship.

Lady is working in Pune, hard working for livelihood. Once took a loan by mortgaging the living house with its plot of land to meet the expenses of family, mainly for treatment of parents. Unexpectedly the pandemic Covid attacked society, outer contacts as well as traffic for work & wages were blocked. So the installments for remittance of Loan became stuck. Children also had to stop study due to lack of fees. Combined with monthly interest Debts also multipled. Bank has started urgent action of immediate foreclosure procedures against pending loans. Kind hearted brethren pls have a merciful attention on this matter, n' take step of sympathetic approach to her pitiful sufferings. Please donate a capable sum of cash as loving gift of humanity to console n' save her in the name of God who provide n' protect us. God's most preferred virtue is charity. Surely all who help the exactly needed poor will be rewarded greately by God.

The charity section of Life Giving News is reaching out with a helping hand for this needy family. For those of you who are willing, invest your money into our bank for this family and you will surely be rewarded with blessings!
