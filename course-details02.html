Holistic Life Changing Training Program

232 Views 35 Comments Dec 01, 2022

Molding a generation of pure spirituality with strong-mindedness determined decisions clear sense of purpose and creative actions. The purpose of this training program is a call from Jesus in the Gospel written by Apostle John. That is, very truly I tell you, whoever believes in me will do the works I have been doing, and they will do even greater things than these, because I am going to the Father.

Be a Royal & Powerful Person

In this way, this training program is for a divine type of evolution in each of us at the physical, mental and spiritual level as desired by Jesus. Moreover, antaryAmi is a holistic life changing training program prepared under the auspices of Life Giving News based on the New Testament word to achieve the quality of life desired by God.

This training program will be useful for anyone who wants to live and act like Jesus. It prepares those who are interested in shaping the next generation by standing in a leadership position and setting their lives on godly standards.

Course Modules:

  • Sonship (Godly Identity Creation Program)
  • Discipleship (Godly Mission Creation Program)
  • Citizenship (Godly Vision Creation Program)
  • Graduateship (Godly Appointment Program)
by Jesus Christ

Believe me that I am in the Father, and the Father in me. I in them and Father in me, that they may become perfectly one, so that the world may know that Father sent me and loved them even as Father loved me.

If I go and prepare a place for you, I will come back and take you to be with me that you also may be where I am. On that day you will know that I am in My Father, and you are in Me, and I in you. Whoever abides in me and I in him, he it is that bears much fruit, for apart from me you can do nothing.

Course Details:

Sonship (Godly Identity Creation Program). It is an 16 hours session, we conduct weekly one online class and completes it in 2 months.

Discipleship (Godly Mission Creation Program). It is a 24 hours session, we conduct weekly one online class and completes it in 3 months.

Citizenship (Godly Vision Creation Program). It is an 10 hours session, we conduct weekly one online class and completes it in 1 month.

Graduateship (Godly Appointment Program). It is an 16 hours session, we conduct 2 days residential direct class to complete it and issues the certificate.
